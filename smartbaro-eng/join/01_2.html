Artificial joint surgery for the hip joint

    This type of surgery inserts an artificialhip joint made of special alloys and ceramics to reduce pain and make joint movements smootherto allow patients to enjoy their daily life in cases where older people’s hip bones have been fracturedfrom slipping on rainy and icy surfaces or distortedfor some other reason, or when ligaments in joints are worn out.

      hip replacement arthroplastry enables patients to walk by themselves and enjoy daily life
