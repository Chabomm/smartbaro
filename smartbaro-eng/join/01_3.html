Artificial Shoulder Arthroplasty

    Artificial shoulder surgery inserts an artificial joint made of a special alloy and polymers when the shoulder joint can not be cured by conservative treatment becausethere has been severe damage done to the shoulder or arthritis or injuries have caused damage.

    Cases for Arthroplasty

    • Arthroplasty is called for when there is degenerative arthritis, necrosis of the head of the humerus avascular, rheumatoid arthritis etc. It is common for older peopleto undergo the surgery. Youngpeople who use their arms a lot also often opt for the surgery.
