Auto-logous Chondrocyte implantation

    Chondrocyte implantation samples auto-logouschnodrocytes and inserts them after culturing them for 2~6weeks in a cell bank and is used in caseswhere the damaged ligament zone is less than 2 cm2.

    This procedure does not engender a rejection response noran adverse reaction because one’s own ligaments are utilized and grafted. The graft survival rate is more than 90 percent.
