Neck/spine disc disorders

    • Cervicogenic headache
      A cervicogenic headache is a pain in which the sensation of pain originates in the cervical spine or soft tissue of the neck and is concentrated in the head.

    • Spinal stenosis
      Spinal stenosis is a medical condition in which the spinal canal narrows and compresses the spinal cord and nerves. Patients are able to bend and lie down and raise their feet while lying down. However, they feel pain when walking as well as pain in the buttocks and legs.
